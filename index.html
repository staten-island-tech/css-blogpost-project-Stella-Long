Deserts

Mango Sago

Mango sago is a creamy, tropical Asian dessert that combines sweet mangoes with chewy tapioca (sago) pearls and coconut milk. This refreshing, chilled dish is widely popular in Hong Kong, and variations are found throughout Southeast Asia, including in Singapore, Malaysia, and the Philippines.

Strawberry cheesecake

Strawberry cheesecake is a classic dessert featuring a creamy, velvety cheesecake filling with a buttery crust, typically graham cracker, and is topped with fresh, ripe strawberries or a sweet strawberry sauce or glaze. It offers a delightful balance of rich, smooth cheesecake flavor with the bright, fresh taste of strawberries, creating a dessert that is both luscious and refreshing.
